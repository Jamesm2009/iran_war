Methodology

This briefing tracks four scenarios for the Iran War 2026, weighted by probability and updated each assessment cycle. Probabilities are research-informed analyst judgement — treat as ordinal rankings, not statistical forecasts. The four-deal-gap framework (uranium, Lebanon/Hezbollah, frozen funds, GOP Senate hawks) provides the structural test: all four must close simultaneously for Scenario 1. The rotating-gap thesis holds that Iran deliberately cycles blocking conditions to buy time against Trump's political clock. Scenario 3 and Scenario 4 are a sequential continuum — Sc3 conditions naturally compound into Sc4 over time.

Maritime data sourced from Windward AI (insights.windward.ai), cached hourly. Market data from CME Group (oil futures), Investing.com (yields), FRED (10-year via API). Country analysis covers: Canada, Colombia, UK, Germany, South Africa, Australia, USA, Ukraine.
